**BSP** (#) General BSP Concept Binary spacing partitioning tree. (#) How BSP works in Quake 1 - Each splitting plane contains several surfaces to be drawn. - BSP recursion stops at leaf. - Unlike what Abrash said in the Graphics Programming Black Book, the recursion was not converted to an iteration. - The leaf doesn't contain surface but indicate the area such as water, lava, solid and empty. It's useful for collision detection. - Test which side of a plane the view point is at P*N-d - Planes behind the view camera will be not drawn thanks to frustum culling - Negative edge index - Frustum culling
